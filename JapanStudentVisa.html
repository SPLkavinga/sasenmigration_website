Documentation & Qualifications for a Japan Student Visa

Qualify for this visa, applicant should be less than 27 years and should have passed G C E Advanced Level. Our students get a unique opportunity to study Japanese in high -tech language classrooms with a highly skilled Japanese language teachers when they get to Japan. The applicant should follow a 2 -year professional Japanese course from the language school before and only after the successful completion of the Japanese course he/she will be able to join a university, vocational /technical college of their choice.
